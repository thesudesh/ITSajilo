Multimedia Computing

This page contains Syllabus of Multimedia Computing of CSIT.

Course Title:Full Marks: 60 + 20 + 20

Course No: CSC314 Pass Marks: 24 + 8 + 8

Nature of the Course: Theory + Lab Credit Hrs: 3

Semester: V


Course Description:

This course covers the basic concepts of Multimedia system including introduction, Sound / Audio System, Images and Graphics,Video and Animation, Data Compression, User Interfaces, Abstractions for programming and Multimedia Application.

Course Objectives:

This course covers three main objectives on multimedia system these are devices, systems and applications.

Units and Unit Content

1.Introduction to Computers teaching hours:(3hrs)

Global Structure of Multimedia; Multimedia Application; Medium; Multimedia System and Properties; Characteristics of a Multimedia System; Challenges for Multimedia Systems;Components of a Multimedia System

2.Sound Audio System teaching hours:(6hrs)

Concepts of Sound System; Music and Speech; Speech Generation;Speech Analysis; Speech Transmission

3.Images and Graphics teaching hours:(5hrs)

Digital Image Representation; Image and graphics Format; Image Synthesis, analysis and Transmission

4.Video and Animation teaching hours:(6hrs)

Video Signal Representation; Computer Video Format; Computer-Based animation; Animation Language; Methods of Controlling Animation; Display of Animation; Transmission of Animation

5.Data Compression teaching hours:(8hrs)

Storage Space; Coding Requirements; Source, Entropy and Hybrid Coding; Lossy Sequential DCT based Mode; Expanded Lossy DCT-based Mode; JPEG and MPEG

6.User Interfaces teaching hours: (5hrs)

Basic Design Issues; Video and Audio at the User Interface; User- friendliness as the Primary Goal

7.Abstractions for programming teaching hours:(5hrs)

8.Multimedia Application teaching hours:(5hrs)

Media Preparation and Composition; Media Integration and Communication; Media Entertainment;Telemedicine; E-learning; Digital Video Editing and Production Systems; Video Conferencing;Video-on-demand

After completing this course, students should have practical knowledge of different components of multimedia system, multimedia tools and applications
